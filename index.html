My Background

Hello! I am currently a student at Dallas College and I am pursuing my Associates degree in Software Development. My goal is to graduate next spring because I am excited to start working in this new field as soon as possible. I have decided to go back to school so that I can create a new career that is challenging and exciting. In the past I have worked in retail and as a receptionist but those careers just didn't seem right for me; I really like working with computers and would love to create a work life around that interest.

When I'm not focusing on school, I really love to create things. Baking, writing, and painting are a few of the things that bring me joy. Since I like to focus on things that make me happy, I also read, watch movies, listen to music, and dance around the house like no one is watching - my dog does judge me though! I hope that once this class is over developing websites will bring me joy too!

I have lived in Texas for over twenty years and have loved it. But I am in an era of trying new things in a big way and I figured why not have a new state to go along with my new career! So I am looking at where I can move to once I graduate. It's overwhelming looking at all of the places I could go but it's also really exciting! I'm really just looking forward to the adventure of it all.

My Web Development Journey

In high school I took a very basic web development class but we did not learn any HTML coding, which I found very disappointing. I have always been very curious as to how websites work behind the scenes so, even though this course is required for my software development degree, I was very excited to see that I would finally be learning how to make my very own websites!

I am even more excited to see what I can do with this new knowledge! I like to write in my spare time so I am looking forward to knowing how to create a space to collect all of my writings. I am also looking forward to the possibility of helping my mom make a website for her consulting business, helping a friend create a website for her tarot reading business, and helping my sister create a place to come together with fans of her books!
